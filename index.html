Biography

I am a graduate student at the Department of Astronomy and Steward Observatory at The University of Arizona in Tucson (AZ). I work with Vasilis Paschalidis on various topics in gravitational physics. In particular, I use and extend the Einstein Toolkit to perform dynamical-spacetime simulations of rotating relativistic stars, and of systems with charged black holes. Moreover, I live inside GNU Emacs.

If you want to know more about me, you can read my academic curriculum vitae or you can send me an email.
